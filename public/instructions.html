Getting Started with Interview Buddy

Welcome to Interview Buddy! We're here to help you ace your next interview with the power of AI. Follow these simple steps to start practicing and boost your confidence.

Step 1: Access the Practice Interface

To begin your practice session, simply navigate to the 'Proceed' button at the bottom of the page.

Step 2: Configure Your Practice Session

Once you're in the configuration interface, you can customize the parameters.

  • Choose Model and Enter your api key of the model. We're working on making this process smoother.
  • Choose Interview Type: Select the specific type of interview you want to practice (Technical, Behavioral).
  • Select Domain: For the Technical Interview you have to choose a specific domain (e.g., Frontend, Backend, DevOps).
  • Set Language: Practice in your preferred language. Our AI supports multiple languages (More will be added).
  • Adjust Time Limits: Opt to set time limits for your answers to simulate real interview pressure.

Step 3: Engage with the AI Interviewer

Now, it's time to put your skills to the test!

  • Listen to the Question: The AI interviewer will clearly present a question to you.
  • Provide Your Answer: Speak clearly and naturally into your microphone. Our seamless speech recognition will transcribe your responses in real-time, allowing you to focus on your delivery.
  • You can seamlessly toggle between text and voice input at any time during the interview.
  • You can toggle the video on and off as needed.
  • If you have the audio mode on then you're not using the text mode. In this case the system will let you pause for 5 seconds after a single utterance i.e for this timeframe the recognition will occur. After that the system will take your response upto that point of time.
  • Receive Instant Feedback: Immediately after your answer, our intelligent AI feedback system will provide constructive criticism. This feedback covers:
    • Clarity: How easy was your answer to understand?
    • Completeness: Did you cover all necessary points comprehensively?
    • Relevance: Was your answer on-topic and appropriate for the question asked?
    • Suggestions for Improvement: Get actionable tips to refine and enhance your answers.
  • After the completion of the interview, you will receive a comprehensive report summarizing your performance, including key strengths and areas for improvement.
  • In case of emergency, you can leave the interview at any time using the "Leave" button. In that case the interview will be considered canceled.

Tips for Best Results

  • Find a Quiet Space: Minimize background noise to ensure optimal speech recognition accuracy.
  • Speak Naturally: Avoid rushing or speaking too slowly. Practice articulating your thoughts as you would in a real interview.
  • Utilize All Features: Experiment with different interview types, domains, and time limits to get a comprehensive practice experience.
  • Don't Be Afraid to Make Mistakes: This is your dedicated practice ground! Every mistake is a valuable learning opportunity that helps you grow.
